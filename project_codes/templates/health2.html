Common Diseases

Influenza (Flu): Symptoms include fever, cough, sore throat, runny or stuffy nose, body aches, headache, chills, and fatigue.

Dengue Fever: Symptoms include high fever, headache, vomiting, muscle and joint pains, and a characteristic skin rash.

Malaria: Symptoms include fever, tiredness, vomiting, and headaches. In severe cases, it can cause yellow skin, seizures, coma, or death.
